services

Food Service/ Food Runner

Food service involves preparing and serving food to customers in a restaurant or catering setting. It includes menu creation, food preparation, customer service, and ensuring a pleasant dining experience. Additionally, it may involve beverage service, catering for events, and adhering to health and safety regulations. Sustainability and marketing are also important considerations.

Refreshment

Refreshments are light snacks and beverages served to quench thirst and provide a brief break during meetings, events, or gatherings. They can include coffee, tea, water, juices, and simple snacks like cookies or sandwiches. Their purpose is to keep participants refreshed and encourage social interaction.

Housekeeping

Housekeeping involves maintaining cleanliness and orderliness in a home, hotel, or similar establishment. It includes tasks like cleaning, tidying, laundry, and organizing to ensure a pleasant and hygienic environment for residents or guests.

Room Security

Room security refers to measures and systems put in place to ensure the safety and protection of occupants and their belongings in a room. This may include locks, keycards, surveillance cameras, and safety procedures to prevent unauthorized access or theft.
